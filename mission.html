Mission & Vision

At Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust, our mission and vision guide every initiative we undertake to empower communities in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh.

Our Mission

To promote holistic development by providing education, healthcare, vocational training, and skill-building opportunities for marginalized communities. We aim to empower individuals to lead dignified, productive, and fulfilling lives.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive society where every individual has access to basic rights, equal opportunities, and resources necessary to achieve their potential. We envision a future free from inequality, poverty, and social injustice.

Core Values

  • Integrity and Transparency in all operations.
  • Empathy and Compassion for every individual.
  • Inclusivity and Social Justice for marginalized groups.
  • Community Participation and Empowerment.
  • Sustainability in development projects.
  • Innovation and Learning for better solutions.
