Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust

About Us

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust is a registered NGO in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities. Our key focus areas include education, healthcare, skill development, legal awareness, and inclusive social welfare programs.

In Loving Memory of Shri Udho Yadav Ji

Shri Udho Yadav Ji

A Visionary Farmer • Social Reformer • Inspiration Behind Our Trust

Shri Udho Yadav Ji was a humble and compassionate individual, dedicated to the service of humanity. His life inspired the creation of this trust to uplift underprivileged communities in education, healthcare, and social welfare. His values guide our mission and continue to shape the trust's initiatives.

The trust carries forward his vision of a society where every individual has access to education, health services, and opportunities to live with dignity. Shri Udho Yadav Ji's legacy is a beacon of hope for generations to come.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To promote holistic development by providing education, healthcare, and skill-building opportunities to those in need.

Vision

To create a society where every individual has access to basic rights, opportunities, and can live a life of dignity.

Key Objectives

  • Education & Literacy
  • Health & Family Welfare
  • Skill Development
  • Legal Awareness & Aid
  • Children & Differently Abled Support
  • Disaster Management
  • Drinking Water & Food Processing
  • Water Resources
  • Prisoner's Issues
