About Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust was founded in memory of Udho Yadav, with a vision to serve society and empower marginalized communities. Based in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, the trust focuses on education, healthcare, skill development, legal awareness, and social welfare. Our aim is to create a society where every individual has access to opportunities, dignity, and a better quality of life.

Founder & Dedication

The trust is dedicated in the name of Udho Yadav, whose vision and compassion for social welfare inspired the foundation of this organization. Every initiative undertaken by the trust reflects his values and commitment to community development.

Our Approach

  • Community engagement through awareness programs and workshops.
  • Providing educational support and scholarships for underprivileged children.
  • Healthcare camps, vaccination drives, and preventive health measures.
  • Vocational and skill development programs for youth and women.
  • Legal aid and awareness for rural communities.
  • Special focus on differently-abled individuals and prisoners’ welfare.
