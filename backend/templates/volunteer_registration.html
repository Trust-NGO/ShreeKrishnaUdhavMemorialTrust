Become a Volunteer

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and help us create positive change in the lives of underserved communities across Deoria, Uttar Pradesh.

Volunteer Guidelines

Who Can Volunteer?
  • Individuals aged 18 years and above
  • Students, professionals, and retirees welcome
  • Must be physically and mentally fit
  • Passionate about social service and community development
Areas of Contribution
  • Education: Teaching, tutoring, and mentoring children
  • Healthcare: Assisting in health camps and awareness programs
  • Administration: Office support, data entry, and communications
  • Field Work: Community outreach and program coordination
  • Skills Training: Conducting vocational workshops
  • Event Management: Organizing fundraising and awareness events
Commitment Expected
  • Minimum 4 hours per week or as per program requirements
  • Attendance at orientation and training sessions
  • Adherence to organizational policies and code of conduct
  • Submission of activity reports where required
Benefits of Volunteering
  • Certificate of appreciation and volunteer hours
  • Skill development and hands-on experience
  • Networking with like-minded individuals
  • Recognition in annual reports and events
  • Personal fulfillment and community impact
All volunteers working with children must undergo background verification as per our Child Protection Policy.
