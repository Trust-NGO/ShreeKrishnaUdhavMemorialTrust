Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?

You can donate online via UPI, bank transfer, or Razorpay on our Donate page. All donations are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G.

Can I volunteer?

Yes! We welcome volunteers. Please fill out the volunteer registration form and our team will contact you.

How are donations used?

Donations fund education, healthcare, skill development, and disaster relief programs. Read our Financial Transparency page for details.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations are eligible for tax exemptions under Section 80G/12A of the Income Tax Act, 1961.
