About Us

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust is a registered NGO dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities through education, healthcare, skill development, and legal awareness. Based in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, we strive for a society where every individual lives with dignity and hope.

Read Our Story

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote holistic development by providing education, healthcare, vocational training, and skill-building opportunities for marginalized communities. We aim to empower individuals to lead dignified, productive, and fulfilling lives.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive society where every individual has access to basic rights, equal opportunities, and resources necessary to achieve their potential. We envision a future free from inequality, poverty, and social injustice.

Why Choose Us

We are committed to transparency, impact, and sustainable community development.

Volunteer Network

Legacy of Service

Founded in memory of Udho Yadav, continuing a legacy of compassion since 2020.

Key Objectives

Education & Literacy

Promote primary, secondary, and adult education, provide scholarships and conduct awareness programs.

Health & Family Welfare

Organize health camps, provide medical aid, nutrition awareness, and maternal & child health services.
