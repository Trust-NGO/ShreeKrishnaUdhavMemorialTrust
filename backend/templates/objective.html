Key Objectives & Focus Areas

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust focuses on several social welfare areas to create a meaningful impact in Deoria and surrounding communities.

Education & Literacy

Establish and operate educational institutions from primary to higher education, including technical, medical, engineering, and computer management colleges. Special emphasis on women's education and scholarships for underprivileged students.

Health & Family Welfare

Organize health camps, provide medical aid, nutrition awareness, maternal & child health services, and promote mental well-being across communities.

Social Welfare & Community Support

Provide assistance for marriages of poor girls, support disabled persons towards self-reliance, establish food banks for the needy, and implement comprehensive development plans for all weaker sections of society.

Legal Awareness & Aid

Promote equality, justice, social harmony, brotherhood, and unity. Provide legal guidance, awareness on constitutional rights, and aid for marginalized groups to ensure justice and equity.

Skill Development

Conduct vocational training, employability programs, and skill-building workshops to empower youth and women economically towards self-reliance.

Sports, Culture & Social Programs

Promote sports and cultural activities, organize social programs on the birth/death anniversaries of great personalities and social reformers who fought against social evils.

Literature & Media

Publish literature, magazines, and maintain web portals for social guidance and awareness. Spread knowledge on education, health, and community development.

Disaster Relief & Special Campaigns

Provide disaster relief and rehabilitation support, lead drug eradication campaigns, run literacy drives, and collaborate with government, non-government, and international organizations for financial aid and grants.
