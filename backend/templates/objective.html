Key Objectives & Focus Areas

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust focuses on several social welfare areas to create a meaningful impact in Deoria and surrounding communities.

Education & Literacy

Promote primary, secondary, and adult education, provide scholarships, and conduct awareness programs to enhance literacy rates.

Health & Family Welfare

Organize health camps, provide medical aid, nutrition awareness, maternal & child health services, and promote mental well-being.

Skill Development

Conduct vocational training, employability programs, and skill-building workshops to empower youth and women economically.

Legal Awareness & Aid

Provide legal guidance, awareness on constitutional rights, and aid for marginalized groups to ensure justice and equity.
