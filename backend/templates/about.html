Our Story

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust was founded on May 23, 2020, by Shri Subhash Chandra Yadav, driven by a profound personal commitment to serve society. The trust stands as an enduring tribute to Krishna Udhav, guided by an unwavering dedication to social welfare, community empowerment, and the dignified upliftment of marginalized communities across India.

Based in Khukhundu, Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, we are a registered trust operating across Entire India. We are dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities through education, healthcare, skill development, and social welfare programs.

Our journey began with a simple belief — that every individual deserves access to education, healthcare, and opportunities for a dignified life. Today, we serve thousands of beneficiaries across Deoria district and beyond through comprehensive programs designed to create lasting change.

Our Approach

How we create meaningful and sustainable change in communities.

Community Engagement

We work directly with communities through awareness programs, participatory workshops, and need assessments to design relevant interventions.

Education & Scholarships

Providing educational support, remedial classes, and scholarships to underprivileged children to improve literacy and reduce dropout rates.

Healthcare & Wellness

Organizing free health camps, vaccination drives, maternal-child health programs, and preventive health awareness in underserved villages.

Skill Development

Vocational training in tailoring, computer literacy, and handicrafts to empower youth and women with employable skills for economic independence.

Legal Awareness

Providing legal aid and awareness on constitutional rights, women's rights, and government welfare schemes for marginalized communities.

Inclusive Welfare

Special focus on differently-abled individuals, prisoner welfare, and rehabilitation programs ensuring no one is left behind.
