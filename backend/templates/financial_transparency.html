Financial Transparency

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust is committed to complete transparency in our financial operations. We believe donors have the right to know how their contributions are utilized.

Registered NGO: Section 8 Company under Companies Act, 2013 | 80G Certified: Tax exemption eligible | DARPAN ID: UP/2024/0406617

1. Our Commitment to Transparency

We maintain the highest standards of financial accountability:

  • Annual financial audits by independent chartered accountants
  • Regular publication of annual reports and financial statements
  • Detailed tracking of every donation from receipt to utilization
  • Compliance with all statutory requirements (Income Tax, ROC, FCRA)
  • Open access to governance documents and registration certificates

2. Fund Utilization Policy

We are committed to ensuring maximum impact from every donation:

Program Expenses (85%)

Direct costs for implementing our charitable programs:

  • Education materials and scholarships
  • Healthcare camps and medical supplies
  • Vocational training equipment and materials
  • Direct beneficiary support
  • Community outreach and awareness
Administrative Costs (15%)

Essential operational expenses:

  • Staff salaries and volunteer support
  • Office rent and utilities
  • Communication and technology
  • Legal and audit fees
  • Travel for program monitoring

7. Donor Rights

As a donor, you have the right to:

  • Know how your donation is being used
  • Receive timely receipts and tax exemption certificates
  • Request information about our programs and impact
  • Visit our project sites (with prior appointment)
  • Opt-out of communications at any time
  • Request a refund in exceptional circumstances (see Refund Policy)
