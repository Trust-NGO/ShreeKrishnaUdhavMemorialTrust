Refund & Donation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust values transparency, accountability, and donor satisfaction in all our fundraising activities. This policy explains the terms, conditions, and procedures regarding donations and refunds.

1. Donation Collection

We accept donations through the following methods:

  • Online payments via Razorpay (Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI, Wallets)
  • Direct Bank Transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS)
  • UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Cheque or Demand Draft (payable to "SHRI KRISHNA UDHAV MEMORIAL TRUST")

All donations are voluntary, non-compulsory, and made with the donor's full consent and understanding of our objectives.

2. Non-Refundable Donations

As a general policy, all donations to Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust are final and non-refundable. Once a donation is processed and acknowledged, it cannot be cancelled or returned, as funds are immediately allocated to our charitable programs.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered only in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Duplicate Payment: If the same donation was accidentally processed multiple times due to technical errors
  • Incorrect Amount: If the donation amount processed differs from the intended amount due to system error
  • Unauthorized Transaction: If the donation was made without the account holder's authorization (subject to verification)
  • Technical Failure: If payment was deducted but donation was not recorded in our system

4. Refund Request Procedure

To request a refund, donors must:

  1. Submit a written refund request via email to krishnaudhavtrust@gmail.com
  2. Include the following details:
    • Donor's full name and contact information
    • Transaction ID / Order ID
    • Date and amount of donation
    • Payment method used
    • Reason for refund request
    • Bank account details for refund (if applicable)
  3. Submit the request within 15 days of the original transaction

5. Refund Processing

  • Refund requests will be reviewed within 7 working days
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 15 working days via the original payment method
  • Bank transfer refunds may take an additional 5-7 working days depending on the bank
  • Any transaction fees charged by payment gateways are non-refundable

6. Tax Benefits & Receipts

  • All donations are eligible for 50% tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961
  • Donation receipts and 80G certificates are issued within 7 working days via email
  • Donors must provide accurate PAN details to claim tax benefits
  • Tax benefits are subject to applicable laws and may change based on government regulations

7. Use of Donations

Donations are used for the following charitable purposes:

  • Education and literacy programs for underprivileged children
  • Healthcare camps, medical aid, and preventive health services
  • Vocational training and skill development for youth and women
  • Legal aid and awareness for marginalized communities
  • Disaster relief and emergency assistance
  • Administrative and operational costs (capped at 15% of total funds)

We maintain transparent financial records and publish annual reports detailing fund utilization.

8. Cancellation Policy

For recurring donations or pledged contributions:

  • Donors may cancel recurring donations by notifying us at least 7 days before the next scheduled payment
  • Cancellation requests must be sent to krishnaudhavtrust@gmail.com
  • Cancellations will take effect from the next billing cycle
  • No refunds for donations already processed

9. Force Majeure

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust shall not be liable for any failure or delay in processing donations or refunds due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including natural disasters, wars, acts of terrorism, government restrictions, or technical failures of third-party payment providers.

10. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from donations or refunds shall be resolved amicably through mutual discussion. If unresolved, disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, India.

11. Record Keeping

We maintain detailed records of all donations and refund transactions for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with the Income Tax Act and other applicable regulations.

12. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Donations made prior to policy changes remain subject to the policy in effect at the time of donation.
