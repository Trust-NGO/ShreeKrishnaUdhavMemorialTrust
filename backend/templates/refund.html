Refund & Donation Policy

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust values transparency and accountability in all our fundraising activities. This Refund & Donation Policy explains the terms and procedures regarding donations.

Donation Collection

Donations are accepted online through UPI, bank transfer, or Paytm. All donations are voluntary and non-refundable unless explicitly mentioned.

Refund Policy

If a donation is mistakenly paid or duplicated, we will review the request and process a refund within 15 working days. Requests must be sent via email with transaction details.

Tax Benefits

All donations are eligible for tax exemptions under Section 80G/12A of the Income Tax Act. Any changes in applicable laws will be updated on the website.

Use of Donations

Donations are used for education, healthcare, skill development, disaster relief, and other community welfare activities as per our NGO objectives.
