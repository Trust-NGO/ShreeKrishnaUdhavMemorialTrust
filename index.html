About Us

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust is a registered NGO dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities through education, healthcare, skill development, and legal awareness. Based in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, we strive for a society where every individual lives with dignity and hope.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities by providing access to quality education, affordable healthcare, and skill-building programs that enable self-reliance. We aim to uplift women, children, and differently-abled individuals, ensuring they have equal opportunities to thrive. Through awareness campaigns, vocational training, and community engagement, we strive to build resilient communities that can face challenges independently.

  • Promote literacy and lifelong learning for all age groups.
  • Ensure access to basic and preventive healthcare services.
  • Empower women and youth through vocational training and skill development.
  • Provide legal guidance and awareness for rights and entitlements.
  • Foster social inclusion and support for differently-abled and marginalized individuals.

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual lives with dignity, security, and equal opportunity. We envision communities where education, health, and livelihood opportunities are accessible to all, and where sustainable development ensures long-term well-being. Our goal is to be a catalyst for social change, bridging gaps between privilege and need, and inspiring collective responsibility towards a more equitable society.

  • Inclusive development for all community members.
  • Bridging the gap between urban and rural education standards.
  • Building self-sufficient and aware communities.
  • Encouraging youth leadership and social entrepreneurship.
  • Creating a sustainable model for community welfare and empowerment.
