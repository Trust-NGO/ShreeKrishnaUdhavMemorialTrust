👤 हमारे बारे में / About Us

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust is a registered NGO in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, working towards the upliftment and empowerment of marginalized communities.
Objectives: To promote education, health & family welfare, skill development and legal awareness, and to address issues faced by children, differently abled, and prisoners.
Sectors: Children, Differently Abled, Disaster Management, Drinking Water, Education & Literacy, Food Processing, Health & Family Welfare, Legal Awareness & Aid, Prisoner's Issues, Water Resources, Skill Development.

Vision / दृष्टि
