About Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust

Founded in the memory of Udho Yadav, Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust is registered and focused on inclusive community development — education, healthcare, skill training, water & sanitation, and legal awareness in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh.

Founding story: Started by local community leaders, the trust was established to channel grassroots efforts into sustainable programs — school scholarships, mobile health camps, women's self-help groups, and vocational training for youth.

Achievements: Over the past years the trust has run multiple health camps, trained local youth in tailoring and agriculture-processing skills, and supported access to government welfare schemes for hundreds of families.

  • Mission

  • Objectives

To promote holistic development by providing education, healthcare, and skill-building opportunities to marginalized communities — focusing on long-term dignity and self-reliance.

To create a society where every individual has access to basic rights, opportunities, and the ability to live a life of dignity, irrespective of background.

  • Education & Literacy: Scholarships, after-school programs, adult-literacy drives.
  • Health & Family Welfare: Free camps, maternal & child health, screenings.
  • Skill Development: Vocational courses — tailoring, food processing, basic IT.
  • Legal Awareness & Aid: Camps to help access entitlements and understand rights.
  • Children & Special Needs: Support & small-scale rehabilitation programs.
  • Disaster & Water Management: Local preparedness and water resource projects.
