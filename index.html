About Us

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust, founded in the memory of Udho Yadav, is dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities through education, healthcare, skill development, and legal awareness. Based in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, we strive for a society where every individual lives with dignity and hope.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote holistic development by providing education, healthcare, and skill-building opportunities to those in need.

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual has access to basic rights, opportunities, and can live a life of dignity.

Key Objectives & Focus Areas

  • Education & Literacy
  • Health & Family Welfare
  • Skill Development
  • Legal Awareness & Aid
  • Children & Differently Abled Support
  • Disaster Management
  • Drinking Water & Food Processing
  • Water Resources
  • Prisoner's Issues
