About Us

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust is a registered NGO in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities through education, healthcare, skill development, and legal awareness. Our goal is to ensure dignity, equality, and hope for all.

Our Mission

Promoting holistic development by providing education, healthcare, and skill-building opportunities for all in need.

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual has access to basic rights, opportunities, and lives with dignity.

Key Objectives

  • Education & Literacy
  • Health & Family Welfare
  • Skill Development
  • Legal Awareness & Aid
  • Children & Differently Abled Support
