Key Objectives & Focus Areas

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust focuses on several social welfare areas to create a meaningful impact in Deoria and surrounding communities.

Education & Literacy

Promote primary, secondary, and adult education, provide scholarships, and conduct awareness programs to enhance literacy rates.

Health & Family Welfare

Organize health camps, provide medical aid, nutrition awareness, maternal & child health services, and promote mental well-being.

Skill Development

Conduct vocational training, employability programs, and skill-building workshops to empower youth and women economically.

Legal Awareness & Aid

Provide legal counseling, awareness programs, and support marginalized communities to access justice.

Children & Differently Abled Support

Offer education, healthcare, rehabilitation, and social support for children and differently-abled individuals.

Disaster Management

Provide relief and rehabilitation support during natural disasters and emergencies.

Drinking Water & Food Processing

Promote access to clean drinking water, sanitation, and support food processing and distribution initiatives.

Water Resources

Focus on water conservation, rainwater harvesting, and development of local water resources.

Prisoner's Issues

Advocate for prisoner welfare, legal rights, and rehabilitation programs for inmates.
