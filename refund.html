Refund & Donation Policy

Shri Krishna Udhav Memorial Trust ensures transparency and proper utilization of all donations received.

Donation Use:

All donations are strictly used for welfare programs including education, health, skill development, and community services in Deoria and nearby areas.

Refunds:

All donations are non-refundable as per NGO practice. Donors are advised to carefully confirm the donation amount before making contributions.

Receipts & Compliance:

Donors will receive official receipts under our 80G / 12A registration for tax benefits.
